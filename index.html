    Hydro-power or water power is power derived from the energy of falling water and running water.

  • Wave power

    Wave power is the transport of energy by ocean surface waves.

  • Geothermal energy

    Geothermal energy is thermal energy generated and stored in the Earth.

    A biofuel is a fuel that is derived from biological materials, such as plants, animals and fish.
